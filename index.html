This is a survey done by June 2018.

Abstract: This survey focuses on tasks about predictions and interactions modeling on Online social media. These tasks can be divided into two categories, post level and conversational level, based on the target items. After the description and analysis of current methods for these tasks, we have found the problems existing, and point out a potential direction for future work.
