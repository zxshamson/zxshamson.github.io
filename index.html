Xingshan Zeng (Hamson)

Xingshan Zeng (曾幸山)

zxshamson AT gmail.com / zeng.xingshan AT huawei.com / Google Scholar / GitHub

I am currently a researcher of Speech and Semantic Group in Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. Before that, I got my PhD in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management (SEEM) Dapartment, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Kam-Fai Wong. I got my B.S. degree from the department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2016.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Social Media Analysis, Recommender Systems and Speech Translation.

I have served as program committee of ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, AACL, AAAI, IJCAI, etc.
